About

I'm a newly minted full stack javaScript Developer with a long history of product design and front end development. I got started in 2004 with a college job at the university of Montana, where students would design and develop websites for faculty, programs and other students. We would use fireworks to design a website and build out the website with HTML, CSS and flash. Since then I've made a career as a graphich designer and web developer sticking primarily to the front end. I've always found my favorite part of my job was the development side, so I recently took a series of courses at Code Fellows in order to improve my development skills and make a career shift into a full time development role.
